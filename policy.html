Our Policy



ENVIRONMENTAL, HEALTH & SAFETY POLICY


GENERAL POLICY STATEMENT

Hilley Consultants Limited is committed to a clean, safe and healthy workplace and environment. We recognize our obligation to take all reasonable precautions to provide and maintain an environment and workplace that is safe and risk free to our employees, customers and the public.

MANAGEMENT COMMITMENT

All levels of Hilley Consultants Limited management are committed to, and accountable for implementing, maintaining, measuring and improving the environmental, health and safety (EHS) programs of the company. In particular, the Technical Manager and Field Technicians shall be responsible and accountable for implementation of EHS programs at the site.

Self-assessments of our performance in this area will be routinely conducted. We will measure performance and hold all employees accountable through performance enhancement processes.

EMPLOYEE WELFARE

The management of Hilley Consultants Limited will continuously provide a safe and healthy work environment for our employees, customers and public. We demand safe work behavior and practices at all times.
The management maintains the policy of equal employment opportunities for all qualified persons and strives to provide all employees with fair equitable terms of employment.
